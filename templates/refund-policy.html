Refund Policy

NeevCode is committed to ensuring customer satisfaction with our services. We understand that circumstances may arise where a refund is requested. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds will be provided.

Free Trial Classes:

  • We offer one (1) free trial class to new members to experience our services. Only one free trial class is permitted per new student.
  • Multiple enrollments in trial classes by an individual, whether through their own account or another account, without prior written permission from NeevCode, are not allowed and will constitute a breach of our terms.
  • Any free trial class provided by NeevCode is subject to the terms outlined in this Refund Policy.

Paid Classes and Subscriptions:

Payments for classes and subscriptions are non-refundable except as outlined below.

  • Refund requests for monthly subscriptions with a limit on the number of classes per month will not be processed.

Refund Requests:

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 45 days of the initial payment.
  • Refund requests can be made by contacting our support team at support@neevcode.com.
  • We aim to process refund requests within 10-15 business days from the date of receiving the request.

App Store Charges:

Any charges related to app publishing, such as those levied by app stores, will be borne by the user and are non-refundable.

Changes to Fees:

NeevCode reserves the right to change fees at any time, with immediate effect upon posting on our website. Please note that any fee changes will not affect ongoing students who are already enrolled in classes or subscriptions at the time of the fee adjustment.

Exclusions:

  • Refunds will not include processing fees, transaction fees, or applicable taxes.
  • Refunds will be subject to deductions for classes already taken by the student. The amount deducted will be calculated based on the number of classes attended or completed at the time of the refund request. Any classes missed by the student without prior notification or invalid reason will be considered as completed classes and will affect the refund amount accordingly. However, if the student provides a valid reason for missing a class and informs us prior to the scheduled class time, the missed class will not affect the refund amount.
  • Refunds may be subject to deductions for cashback, discounts, or rewards received at the time of purchase.

Financed Courses:

The refund timeline and process for financed courses are subject to the terms and conditions agreed upon with the third-party financing entity.

Dispute Resolution:

Any disputes regarding refunds will be handled in accordance with our Dispute Resolution process outlined in our Terms and Conditions.

Contact Us:

For any inquiries or assistance regarding refund requests, please contact our support team at support@neevcode.com.

This Refund Policy is subject to change without prior notice. Please review it periodically for any updates.
